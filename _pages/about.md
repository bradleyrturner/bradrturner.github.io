**On the job market · 2025–2026**

I am a doctoral candidate in Economic Sociology at the MIT Sloan School of Management. My research examines how entrepreneurs and organizations use narratives to persuade and coordinate stakeholders, with empirical work in private equity and financial markets.

My work has been published in the *Academy of Management Annals*. Before MIT, I held a Fulbright Fellowship in philosophy at the University of Helsinki, worked at Bridgewater Associates and Moody's, and managed a lab at Cornell-Johnson. I hold an M.A. in Sociology from the University of Chicago and a B.S. in Economics from Syracuse University.

I am on the academic job market this year. Please feel free to [reach out](mailto:bradley1@mit.edu).
